# PPO_18 Design

**Date:** 2026-02-27
**Baseline:** PPO_17 (eval peak 24.17, settled 18–22, 2M steps)

## Goal

Push past PPO_17's ceiling on all three axes: higher eval score, less volatile eval, better chain-building behavior.

## Diagnosis of PPO_17

| Signal | Value | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Eval peak | 24.17 @ ~1.3M steps | Hit ceiling early, plateaued |
| Entropy (final) | −3.68 (from −4.09) | Declined too fast; policy hardened before optimum |
| Clip fraction (final) | 0.142 (from 0.023) | LR too high late; updates too aggressive |
| Policy gradient loss (final) | −0.0075 (from −0.025) | Shrinking signal; local optimum |
| Rollout/eval divergence | Rollout DOWN, eval UP late | Mild reward shaping exploitation |

## Changes

### 1. Reward: Reduce `adjacent_patterns_created` weight

```python
# PPO_17
reward += adjacent_patterns_created * 0.10

# PPO_18
reward += adjacent_patterns_created * 0.05
```

Rationale: Equal weight to `patterns_created`. Sufficient signal to prefer chain-extending placements without dominating the reward and encouraging exploitation of the combo zone heuristic.

No other reward changes. Bumpiness shaping was considered and rejected — scaffolding strategies (build partial structure, fill in to complete wide chain) legitimately produce non-flat boards.

### 2. Hyperparameters

| Parameter | PPO_17 | PPO_18 | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| `final_ent` | 0.01 | 0.03 | Keep more exploration through late training |
| LR schedule final | 3e-6 | 1e-6 | Reduce late-training update size |
| `total_timesteps` | 2M | 3M | PPO_17 peaked at 1.3M; extra 1M to push past plateau |

### 3. No architecture changes

Architecture is healthy (EV=0.70, KL clean). 4-channel CNN + MLP with `share_features_extractor=False` stays unchanged.

## Implementation

Two files change:

**`python/game/env.py`**
- Line: `adjacent_patterns_created * 0.10` → `* 0.05`

**`python/train.py`**
- `EntropyScheduleCallback(initial_ent=0.1, final_ent=0.01, ...)` → `final_ent=0.03`
- `linear_schedule(3e-5, 3e-6)` → `linear_schedule(3e-5, 1e-6)`
- Default `--timesteps` 2_000_000 → 3_000_000

## Success Criteria

- Eval peak > 24.17 (beat PPO_17 all-time best)
- Eval variance tighter (last 10 evals std dev < PPO_17's)
- No eval collapse (PPO_10/11 pattern of sharp decline after peak)
